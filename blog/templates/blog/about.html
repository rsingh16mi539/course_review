About "Course Review"


Course Review is a web application through which student can review a course as well as share their experience about the course and the course coordinator.


The objectives of this project is to provide a helping hand to the students who would like to prepare well in advance for their respective courses which they look forward to take in their upcoming semester. This will also help students to manage their time as they will have prior knowledge about the various activities that course coordinator organizes throughout the course.
